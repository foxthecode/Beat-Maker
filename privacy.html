Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Kick & Snare ("the App") respects your privacy. All audio processing, sampling, and sequencing are performed locally on your device.

  • Microphone Access: Used exclusively for the live sampling feature. No audio data is recorded without user action, and no data is transmitted to external servers.
  • Storage Access: Used only to import your local sound samples and export your beats.
  • Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or usage data.
